My Story

A DFW Local Who Actually Lives It

I'm Kristen Carpentier — a licensed Texas Realtor®, a mom of four, and a Flower Mound resident for over 14 years. My husband is an Army Veteran, and our family has planted deep roots right here in North DFW. This isn't just where I work — it's home.

I know what it feels like to move your family to a new city. The anxiety of getting the schools wrong. The overwhelm of staring at a map with 60 suburbs and no idea which one fits your family. The fear of making a $500,000 decision with incomplete information. I've lived it firsthand — and I've spent the last decade helping hundreds of other families navigate that exact same journey.

I started the Why Move to Dallas YouTube channel because I kept meeting families online who were drowning in generic information. They needed someone to be honest with them about the real differences between Frisco and Flower Mound, between Southlake and Colleyville, between what a suburb looks like on paper and what it's actually like to raise kids there. So I started making those videos — and thousands of relocating families found them.

My approach has always been education first. I'm not going to rush you into a decision. I'm going to help you understand exactly why a specific suburb is right — or wrong — for your family, and I'll tell you the honest tradeoffs every time.

I specialize exclusively in DFW family relocation. I'm not a generalist who covers all of Texas. I know every suburb, every school district, every commute corridor, and every tradeoff between them. When you work with me, you get someone who's done her homework — and done this move herself.

My Approach

How I Help Families Relocate to DFW

I don't start with listings. I start with your family's priorities — then build everything else around that.

1

Understand What Matters Most

Before we look at a single house, we talk. Schools, commute, budget, timeline, neighborhood feel — I map out your priorities and make sure I understand what the right move actually looks like for your family specifically.

2

Match You to the Right Suburb

Based on your priorities, I narrow 60+ suburbs down to the 2–3 that are the strongest fit — and explain exactly why each one does or doesn't work. No guessing. No pressure. Just honest suburb-by-suburb analysis.

3

Search with Full Market Knowledge

With your suburb identified, we search with complete local expertise: resale homes, new construction builders, what's a great value vs. what's overpriced, and what's likely to appreciate. I know every community, every builder, and every tradeoff.

4

Close — and Stay Available

I guide you through offer, inspection, and closing — and I stay available after the keys are in your hand. My clients come back to me years later with suburb questions, school enrollment questions, contractor referrals. That's the relationship I build.

Who I Work With

Five Families I Help Every Day

Every client's situation is different — but most fall into one of these five profiles. If any of these sounds like you, you're in the right place.

Corporate Transferee

Employer-sponsored relo to Toyota, American Airlines, JPMorgan, or another DFW headquarters. You have 60 days, school-age kids, and no idea which suburb is right. I've helped dozens of families in this exact situation — fast.

Timeline: 30–60 days

CA / NY / IL Escapee

Leaving California, New York, or Illinois for lower taxes, more space, and a better cost of living. You know DFW is the right move — you just need someone to help you pick the right neighborhood for your family inside a metro of 8 million people.

Timeline: 3–6 months

Remote Worker Upgrade

You can live anywhere — and you're choosing DFW for the lifestyle, the weather, the affordability, or to be near family. You have time to be thorough and you want to get this exactly right. That's my favorite kind of search.

Timeline: 6–12 months

Growing DFW Family

Already in DFW but outgrowing your current home or neighborhood. You know the metro — you just need help finding the right suburb with the schools and space your expanding family needs. I know every neighborhood from the inside.

Timeline: 3–9 months

Military Family

PCS orders to NAS Fort Worth JRB, or a veteran settling in DFW post-service. No state income tax, strong job market, VA-friendly communities — DFW has a lot going for it. My husband served in the Army. I understand tight timelines and school stability concerns personally.

Timeline: 30–60 days

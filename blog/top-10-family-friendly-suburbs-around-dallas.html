Top 10 Family-Friendly Suburbs Around Dallas

Kristen Carpentier Licensed TX Realtor®  ·  May 30, 2024  ·  15 min read

Relocating to the Dallas metro area is an exciting opportunity, especially for families seeking the best environments to raise their children. The right suburb can offer excellent schools, safe neighborhoods, and many activities for kids and parents alike. Here are the top 10 family-friendly suburbs around Dallas, chosen for their exceptional qualities that make them ideal for families.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="1280"] Dallas Family Friendly Suburbs Comparison Chart 2024 [/caption]

1. Plano

Education Excellence in Plano Texas

Plano is renowned for its exceptional education system. The Plano Independent School District (PISD) consistently ranks among the top in Texas, offering a range of programs and resources that cater to all students, from elementary to high school. According to Niche.com, PISD has an overall A+ rating and is ranked #13 among the Best School Districts in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. With a focus on academic achievement and extracurricular opportunities, Plano ensures that children receive a well-rounded education, preparing them for future success.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="2162"] Plano ISD niche.com ratings 2024 [/caption]

Safety and Security in Plano Texas

Safety is a top priority for families, and Plano does not disappoint. The city boasts low crime rates and a strong community policing initiative, making it one of the safest places to live in the Dallas metro area. According to NeighborhoodScout, Plano's crime rate is significantly lower than the national average, with a crime index of 35, meaning it is safer than 35% of U.S. cities. Parents can feel confident that their children are growing up in a secure environment, allowing them to explore and play freely.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="1362"] Plano Texas Crime Ratings 2024 from BestPlaces.net [/caption]

Recreational Activities in Plano Texas

When it comes to recreational activities, Plano has plenty to offer. The city is home to numerous parks, playgrounds, and family-friendly events throughout the year. Arbor Hills Nature Preserve and Oak Point Park provide vast green spaces for hiking, biking, and picnicking. Additionally, the city hosts cultural festivals, farmers' markets, and seasonal events that bring the community together. From outdoor adventures to cultural celebrations, there’s always something happening in Plano that the whole family can enjoy.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="1200"] Arbor Hills Nature Preserve [/caption]

2. Frisco

Education Opportunities

Frisco is a top choice for families, thanks in part to its outstanding education system. Frisco Independent School District (FISD) is known for its modern facilities and high academic standards. Niche.com gives FISD an A+ rating, ranking it #8 among the Best School Districts in the Dallas-Fort Worth area and #15 in Texas. The district offers a variety of specialized programs, including STEM, arts, and advanced placement courses, ensuring that students have access to a broad and challenging curriculum. With a strong emphasis on student success, Frisco schools provide an excellent environment for children to thrive academically and personally.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="2162"] Frisco ISD Ratings niche.com 2024 [/caption]

Sports and Recreation

Frisco is a haven for sports enthusiasts. The city features world-class sports facilities, including the Ford Center at The Star, home of the Dallas Cowboys' headquarters, and Toyota Stadium, home to FC Dallas. Youth sports programs are abundant, offering opportunities in soccer, football, baseball, and more. Frisco's numerous parks and recreation centers also provide spaces for families to enjoy outdoor activities, from playgrounds to hiking trails. Whether your family enjoys playing or watching sports, Frisco offers abundant opportunities for both.

Community Events

One of the highlights of living in Frisco is the plethora of family-friendly events. The city hosts various cultural and seasonal festivals that bring the community together. Events like the Frisco Freedom Fest, Merry Main Street, and Frisco Arts Walk and Run create opportunities for families to bond and make lasting memories. These events not only entertain but also foster a strong sense of community, making Frisco a welcoming and vibrant place to live.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="1200"] Frisco Freedomfest [/caption]

3. Southlake

Southlake Texas has Top-Rated Schools

Southlake’s Carroll Independent School District (CISD) is a significant draw for families. Known for its academic excellence, CISD consistently ranks among the best in Texas. Niche.com rates CISD as the #1 Best School District in the Dallas-Fort Worth area, with an overall grade of A+. The district offers advanced programs and a supportive learning environment, ensuring that students receive a top-tier education. Southlake schools are also known for their strong emphasis on athletics and extracurricular activities, providing students with a balanced and enriching educational experience.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="2170"] Carroll ISD School Ratings niche.com 2024 [/caption]

Southlake is an Affluent Community

Southlake offers a high standard of living, with upscale amenities and services that appeal to families seeking a luxurious lifestyle. The suburb features beautiful homes, excellent shopping, and fine dining options, making it a desirable place to live. Southlake Town Square is a central hub for shopping, dining, and entertainment, offering a mix of high-end retailers and local boutiques. This affluent community attracts families who value quality living and access to premium services.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="2200"] Southlake Town Center [/caption]

Outdoor Activities in Southlake Texas

For families who love the outdoors, Southlake has plenty to offer. The city boasts numerous nature trails, parks, and recreational sports leagues. Bob Jones Nature Center and Preserve is a popular spot for hiking, bird watching, and exploring nature. Southlake’s parks are well-maintained and offer a range of amenities, including sports fields, playgrounds, and picnic areas. Whether you enjoy hiking, biking, or playing in the park, Southlake provides ample opportunities for outdoor fun.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="1200"] Bob Jones Nature Preserve [/caption]

4. McKinney

Historic Charm of McKinney Texas

McKinney’s historic downtown area sets it apart from other suburbs. The charming streets, heritage sites, and local museums create a unique atmosphere that families find appealing. The downtown area is lined with preserved 19th-century buildings, housing boutique shops, restaurants, and galleries. McKinney’s commitment to preserving its history while fostering modern development creates a charming blend of old and new, making it a delightful place to call home.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="1000"] McKinney Texas Downtown Christmas [/caption]

McKinney Texas is a Growing Community

McKinney is one of the fastest-growing communities in the Dallas metro area. With new residential developments and community growth initiatives, the suburb offers modern living while maintaining its small-town charm. The city has seen significant investment in infrastructure, schools, and public amenities, supporting its growing population. This growth ensures that families have access to new amenities and services, making McKinney a dynamic and evolving place to live.

Family-Oriented Amenities in McKinney Texas

McKinney is rich in family-oriented amenities. The suburb features community centers, libraries, and a variety of family-friendly dining and shopping options. The Apex Centre, for example, offers a range of recreational activities, including swimming, fitness classes, and sports. Additionally, McKinney hosts numerous family-focused events, such as the McKinney Oktoberfest and the Arts in Bloom festival, providing ample opportunities for community engagement and family fun.

5. Flower Mound

Quality Education in Flower Mound Texas

Flower Mound is served by the highly regarded Lewisville Independent School District (LISD). The district is known for its excellent schools and diverse extracurricular activities, providing children with numerous opportunities to excel both academically and socially. Niche.com rates LISD with an overall grade of A, highlighting its commitment to student success and well-rounded education. LISD offers programs in the arts, athletics, and advanced academics, ensuring a comprehensive educational experience.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="2152"] Lewisville ISD niche.com Ratings 2024 [/caption]

Green Spaces in Flower Mound Texas

One of Flower Mound’s standout features is its extensive green spaces. The suburb is home to numerous parks and nature preserves, offering families plenty of options for outdoor recreation. Murrell Park and the Flower Mound Trail System provide miles of trails for hiking, biking, and horseback riding. These green spaces are not only beautiful but also foster a sense of community and well-being, making Flower Mound an ideal place for nature lovers.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="1792"] Flower Mound Texas Trail System [/caption]

Community Engagement

Flower Mound fosters a strong sense of community through active neighborhood associations and family-friendly events. The suburb’s Parks and Recreation Department hosts a variety of programs and events throughout the year, including concerts in the park, holiday celebrations, and community festivals. These initiatives help create a welcoming environment where families can connect, participate, and thrive together.

6. Coppell

Education Excellence in Coppell Texas

Coppell Independent School District (CISD) is a major attraction for families moving to the area. Known for its high academic standards and focus on STEM education, CISD ensures that students receive a comprehensive and forward-thinking education. Niche.com gives CISD an A+ rating, ranking it as one of the top school districts in Texas. The district offers various advanced placement courses, gifted and talented programs, and extracurricular activities that cater to diverse student interests and strengths. This strong educational foundation prepares students for success in higher education and beyond.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="2160"] Coppell ISD Ratings niche.com 2024 [/caption]

Safe Community in Coppell Texas

Coppell is renowned for its safety, boasting low crime rates and active neighborhood watch programs. According to NeighborhoodScout, Coppell has a crime index of 60, meaning it is safer than 60% of U.S. cities. The city’s police department works closely with the community to maintain a secure environment, implementing initiatives like community policing and crime prevention programs. This secure environment provides peace of mind for parents, knowing their children are growing up in a safe and supportive community.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="1368"] Coppell Texas Crime Rating BestPlaces.net 2024 Safer Than Frisco Texas [/caption]

Family Activities in Coppell Texas

Coppell offers a variety of family activities, from parks and recreation centers to community events and activities. The Coppell Aquatic and Recreation Center is a popular spot for families, offering swimming pools, fitness classes, and sports leagues. The city also hosts seasonal events, such as the Coppell Farmers Market and holiday parades, providing opportunities for families to come together and enjoy quality time. These amenities ensure that there is always something fun and engaging for families to do together.

These recreational options encourage physical activity and provide opportunities for families to enjoy outdoor fun together.

Family-Centric Community

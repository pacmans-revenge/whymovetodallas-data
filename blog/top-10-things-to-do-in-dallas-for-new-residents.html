Top 10 Things to Do in Dallas for New Residents

Kristen Carpentier Licensed TX Realtor®  ·  May 31, 2024  ·  23 min read

Moving to a new city can be both exciting and overwhelming. Dallas, with its vibrant culture, rich history, and plethora of activities, offers a warm welcome to new residents. Whether you're a history buff, a foodie, an outdoor enthusiast, or a family with young children, Dallas has something to offer for everyone. Here's a comprehensive guide to the top 10 things to do in Dallas for new residents.

Iconic Dallas Attractions

The Sixth Floor Museum at Dealey Plaza

The Sixth Floor Museum at Dealey Plaza is an essential visit for history enthusiasts. This museum is located in the former Texas School Book Depository building and chronicles the life, death, and legacy of President John F. Kennedy. Visitors can explore exhibits featuring photographs, artifacts, and documentary films that provide an in-depth look into one of the most significant events in American history.

Reunion Tower

Reunion Tower, one of Dallas's most recognizable landmarks, offers a unique perspective of the city. The observation deck, known as the GeO-Deck, provides panoramic views of the Dallas skyline. Whether you visit during the day or night, the breathtaking views are unforgettable. The interactive exhibits and telescopes enhance the experience, making it a must-visit for new residents and tourists alike.

Dallas Arboretum and Botanical Garden

For a serene escape from the bustling city, the Dallas Arboretum and Botanical Garden is a perfect destination. Spanning 66 acres, this lush garden offers a stunning display of seasonal flowers, trees, and plants. Special events, such as the Dallas Blooms festival and the autumn pumpkin patch, provide delightful experiences throughout the year. The tranquil environment makes it an ideal spot for picnics, leisurely strolls, and photography.

Perot Museum of Nature and Science

The Perot Museum of Nature and Science is an engaging and educational attraction for all ages. The museum features interactive exhibits on topics ranging from dinosaurs and space exploration to human biology and engineering. The state-of-the-art facility includes a 3D theater, a children’s museum, and a unique outdoor science park. It's a fantastic place for new residents to learn about science and technology while having fun.

Klyde Warren Park

Klyde Warren Park is an urban oasis in the heart of Dallas. Built over a freeway, this innovative park connects downtown Dallas with the uptown neighborhood. It offers a variety of activities and amenities, including food trucks, a dog park, children's playgrounds, and performance stages. Regular events, such as yoga classes, live music, and movie nights, make it a vibrant community hub where new residents can meet and socialize.

Outdoor Activities and Parks

White Rock Lake

White Rock Lake is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. This 1,015-acre reservoir offers a range of recreational activities, including boating, fishing, hiking, and biking. The 9-mile trail around the lake is perfect for jogging or cycling, providing picturesque views of the water and surrounding greenery. The park also features picnic areas, bird-watching opportunities, and the Dallas Arboretum on its shores.

Cedar Ridge Preserve

Cedar Ridge Preserve is a natural haven just a short drive from downtown Dallas. With 600 acres of preserved wilderness, it offers a network of hiking trails that wind through scenic hills, forests, and meadows. The preserve is home to diverse wildlife and native plants, making it a great spot for nature lovers and birdwatchers. The well-maintained trails vary in difficulty, catering to both casual hikers and more experienced trekkers.

Trinity River Audubon Center

The Trinity River Audubon Center provides a unique outdoor experience within the city limits. Situated in the Great Trinity Forest, the largest urban hardwood forest in the United States, this center offers educational programs, nature trails, and bird-watching opportunities. The eco-friendly building features exhibits on local ecology and conservation efforts. It's an ideal place for families and individuals to connect with nature and learn about environmental stewardship.

Katy Trail

The Katy Trail is a beloved urban trail that stretches for 3.5 miles through the heart of Dallas. Following the path of an old railroad line, the trail is popular with walkers, joggers, and cyclists. It connects various neighborhoods, parks, and cultural sites, making it a convenient and scenic route for exploring the city. The trail's well-maintained surface and shaded sections provide a comfortable experience year-round.

Dallas Zoo

The Dallas Zoo, one of the largest and oldest zoos in Texas, offers an enjoyable day out for families and animal lovers. Spanning 106 acres, the zoo is home to over 2,000 animals representing more than 400 species. Popular exhibits include the Giants of the Savanna, the Wilds of Africa, and the Children's Zoo. The zoo also hosts special events and educational programs, providing interactive and entertaining experiences for visitors of all ages.

Family-Friendly Activities

Dallas World Aquarium

The Dallas World Aquarium is a fascinating destination for families. Located in the West End Historic District, the aquarium features exhibits that simulate various ecosystems, from the Orinoco Rainforest to the South African Coast. Visitors can see a wide range of marine and terrestrial animals, including sharks, penguins, and exotic birds. The aquarium's interactive displays and feeding shows make it an engaging experience for children and adults alike.

LEGOLAND Discovery Center

LEGOLAND Discovery Center in Grapevine Mills Mall is a paradise for LEGO enthusiasts. This indoor attraction features interactive rides, a 4D cinema, and extensive LEGO building zones. Kids can explore themed areas like LEGO Ninjago City Adventure and Miniland, which showcases iconic Dallas landmarks made from LEGO bricks. It's a perfect place for creative play and imaginative exploration.

Dallas Children's Theater

The Dallas Children's Theater offers high-quality theatrical productions specifically designed for young audiences. The theater presents a diverse range of performances, from classic fairy tales to contemporary stories, often incorporating music, dance, and puppetry. With its engaging and educational shows, the Dallas Children's Theater provides an excellent cultural experience for families.

Epic Waters Indoor Waterpark

Epic Waters Indoor Waterpark in Grand Prairie is a year-round destination for water fun. The park features a retractable roof, making it a perfect spot regardless of the weather. With numerous slides, a lazy river, a wave pool, and a children's play area, Epic Waters offers excitement for all ages. Special events and themed nights add to the fun, ensuring a memorable visit every time.

Frontiers of Flight Museum

The Frontiers of Flight Museum, located at Dallas Love Field, offers a captivating journey through aviation history. The museum's extensive collection includes vintage aircraft, spacecraft, and aviation artifacts. Interactive exhibits and educational programs make it an engaging experience for children and adults. Highlights include the Apollo 7 command module and the Chance Vought V-173 "Flying Pancake." It's a must-visit for anyone interested in the history of flight.

Best Restaurants and Eateries

Dallas's diverse culinary landscape ensures that new residents and visitors alike can find an array of dining options to suit any palate. Whether you're in the mood for traditional Texas barbecue, innovative Japanese cuisine, or elegant French dining, Dallas has a restaurant that will exceed your expectations. Each eatery offers a unique experience, contributing to the rich tapestry of the city's food scene.

Pecan Lodge

Location: Deep Ellum

Pecan Lodge is a legendary barbecue spot in the Deep Ellum neighborhood of Dallas. Known for its mouthwatering smoked meats, this restaurant has become a staple for both locals and visitors. The brisket, ribs, and sausages are slow-cooked to perfection, resulting in tender, flavorful meats that have earned national acclaim.

Signature Dishes:

- Brisket: Smoky, tender, and packed with flavor.

- Hot Mess: A heaping sweet potato stuffed with barbacoa, cheese, chipotle cream, and green onions.

- Beef Rib: A massive rib with a perfect bark and melt-in-your-mouth meat.

Pecan Lodge’s casual atmosphere, with communal tables and a rustic vibe, makes it a great place for a laid-back meal with friends or family. Expect long lines, but the wait is worth it for a true Texas barbecue experience.

Uchi Dallas

Location: Uptown

Uchi Dallas offers an exquisite dining experience with its innovative Japanese cuisine. This upscale restaurant blends traditional Japanese techniques with contemporary flavors, resulting in visually stunning and delicious dishes.

Signature Dishes:

- Sake Toro (Salmon Belly): Rich and buttery, perfect for sushi enthusiasts.

- Machi Cure: Smoked yellowtail with yucca chips, Marcona almonds, and Asian pear.

- Hot Rock: Wagyu beef served with a searing hot stone for cooking at the table.

The sleek, modern décor and intimate atmosphere make Uchi popular for special occasions and romantic dinners. The omakase (chef’s tasting menu) is a great way to experience a curated selection of the restaurant's best offerings.

Lockhart Smokehouse

Location: Bishop Arts District and Plano

Lockhart Smokehouse brings the flavors of Central Texas barbecue to Dallas. Known for its authentic approach, the restaurant smokes its meats over Texas post oak wood and serves them on butcher paper, which is true to traditional barbecue methods.

Signature Dishes:

- Brisket: Juicy and flavorful, with a perfect smoke ring.

- Kreuz Market Sausages: Spicy and savory, sourced directly from Lockhart, Texas.

- Beef Ribs: Meaty and tender, with a robust smoky flavor.

The laid-back, communal seating and relaxed atmosphere create a welcoming environment where guests enjoy hearty portions of classic Texas barbecue.

Mi Cocina

Location: Various locations throughout Dallas

Mi Cocina is a beloved Tex-Mex chain offering vibrant flavors and a festive atmosphere. With several locations across Dallas, it’s a convenient choice for both casual dining and special gatherings.

Signature Dishes:

- Sunset Fajitas: Marinated and grilled to perfection, served with fresh tortillas and all the fixings.

- Tacos “De Brisket”: Tender brisket with savory spices, wrapped in warm tortillas.

- Mambo Taxi: A frozen margarita with a swirl of sangria, a favorite among patrons.

Mi Cocina’s lively environment, colorful décor, and attentive service make it a go-to spot for families, friends, and celebrations. The extensive menu ensures something for everyone, from classic enchiladas to innovative Tex-Mex creations.

The French Room

Location: Adolphus Hotel, Downtown

The French Room, located in the historic Adolphus Hotel, is a fine dining institution in Dallas. Known for its opulent décor and exquisite French cuisine, this restaurant offers a truly luxurious dining experience.

Signature Dishes:

- Foie Gras Torchon: Rich and creamy, served with seasonal accompaniments.

- Duck à l'Orange: A classic French dish with a perfect balance of flavors.

- Grand Marnier Soufflé: Light and airy, a perfect end to a refined meal.

The French Room’s crystal chandeliers, ornate moldings, and elegant table settings create a sophisticated ambiance, making it ideal for special occasions and formal dinners. The impeccable service and attention to detail ensure a memorable dining experience.

Rise No. 1

Location: Inwood Village

Rise No. 1 is a charming French bistro known for its signature soufflés. The cozy, cottage-like setting and whimsical décor make it a unique and delightful dining place.

Signature Dishes:

- Cheese Soufflé: Light, fluffy, and bursting with rich cheese flavor.

- Crab Soufflé: A seafood twist on the classic, with tender crab meat.

- Chocolate Soufflé: Decadent and indulgent, a must-try for dessert lovers.

In addition to soufflés, the menu features a variety of French-inspired dishes, including salads, soups, and quiches. The warm, inviting atmosphere and friendly service make Rise No. 1 a favorite for casual meals and special treats.

Fearing's Restaurant

Location: Ritz-Carlton, Uptown

Fearing's Restaurant, located in the luxurious Ritz-Carlton, offers an upscale dining experience focusing on modern Southwestern cuisine. Chef Dean Fearing’s innovative dishes, using locally sourced ingredients, highlight the best of Texas flavors.

Signature Dishes:

- Tortilla Soup: A flavorful, hearty soup with a perfect blend of spices.

- Chicken-Fried Maine Lobster: A unique twist on a classic with rich, buttery lobster meat.

- Dr Pepper Braised Short Ribs: Tender and savory, with a sweet and tangy glaze.

The elegant yet comfortable setting and exceptional service make Fearing's a top choice for fine dining in Dallas. The restaurant's various dining areas, including the lively Rattlesnake Bar and the serene Ocaso patio, provide diverse atmospheres for any occasion.

Café Momentum

Location: Downtown

Café Momentum is not only known for its delicious food but also for its inspiring mission. This nonprofit restaurant provides a transformative experience for at-risk youth through culinary training and life skills development. The seasonal menu features locally sourced ingredients and creative dishes.

Signature Dishes:

- Roasted Chicken: Perfectly cooked with a crispy skin and tender meat.

- Shrimp and Grits: Creamy grits topped with succulent shrimp and flavorful sauce.

- Seasonal Cheesecake: Made with fresh, local ingredients, changing seasons.

Dining at Café Momentum allows guests to enjoy a fantastic meal while supporting a worthy cause. The contemporary décor and positive atmosphere create a welcoming environment for all.

Tei-An

Location: One Arts Plaza, Downtown

Tei-An is an upscale Japanese restaurant specializing in soba noodles and offering a sophisticated menu of Japanese delicacies. The minimalist, elegant setting and the meticulous attention to detail in the food and presentation make it a standout dining experience.

Signature Dishes:

- Soba Noodles: Handcrafted and served in various traditional and modern styles.

- Uni Pasta: Creamy and rich, combining the flavors of sea urchin with al dente pasta.

- Omakase: A chef’s tasting menu highlighting the best seasonal ingredients.

Tei-An’s serene ambiance and exceptional service provide a tranquil dining experience, perfect for those seeking a refined and authentic taste of Japan in Dallas.

Smoky Rose

Location: Near White Rock Lake

Smoky Rose is a charming smokehouse and garden restaurant offering a relaxed dining experience focusing on smoked meats and Southern cuisine. The rustic yet modern setting, complete with a beautiful outdoor garden area, makes it a great spot for casual dining.

Signature Dishes:

- Smoked Brisket: Tender and juicy, with a flavorful bark.

- Pulled Pork Sandwich: Savory pulled pork topped with tangy coleslaw.

- Smoked Chicken: Moist and flavorful, with a perfect smoky finish.

The inviting atmosphere, delicious food, and friendly service make Smoky Rose a popular choice for locals and visitors looking to enjoy hearty, comforting meals.

Lucia

Location: Bishop Arts District

Lucia is a small, intimate Italian restaurant in the Bishop Arts District, known for its house-made pasta and artisanal charcuterie. The cozy setting and limited seating create an exclusive dining experience, making reservations highly recommended.

Signature Dishes:

- Pappardelle Bolognese: Rich and hearty, with tender pasta and savory meat sauce.

- Duck Leg Confit: Crispy on the outside, tender and flavorful on the inside.

- Tiramisu: Classic Italian dessert with layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers and creamy mascarpone.

Lucia’s focus on quality ingredients and traditional Italian techniques and its warm, intimate ambiance make it a beloved spot for Italian cuisine enthusiasts.

Town Hearth

Location: Design District

Town Hearth is a lively steakhouse in the Design District known for its opulent décor and indulgent menu. The restaurant features a dramatic interior with crystal chandeliers, vintage motorcycles, and an open kitchen.

Signature Dishes:

- Bone-In Ribeye: Perfectly cooked to order, with a rich, buttery flavor.

- Lobster Thermidor: Decadent and creamy, a luxurious seafood option.

- Duck Fat Potatoes: Crispy and flavorful, a perfect side dish.

The energetic atmosphere, exceptional food, and unique setting make Town Hearth a top destination for steak lovers and those looking to celebrate special occasions in style.

Nobu Dallas

Location: Crescent Court, Uptown

Nobu Dallas, part of the renowned Nobu restaurant group, offers a world-class dining experience with its fusion of traditional Japanese dishes and Peruvian ingredients. The chic, modern décor and impeccable service make it a go-to spot for sushi enthusiasts and those seeking innovative cuisine.

Signature Dishes:

- Black Cod Miso: A buttery, melt-in-your-mouth dish that is a signature of Nobu restaurants worldwide.

- Yellowtail Jalapeño: Fresh sashimi with a zesty kick.

- Rock Shrimp Tempura: Crispy and flavorful, served with a creamy spicy sauce.

Nobu's extensive menu and vibrant atmosphere provide an exceptional dining experience, perfect for special occasions or a sophisticated night out.

Gemma

Location: Knox-Henderson

Gemma offers a cozy yet upscale dining experience focusing on seasonal, contemporary American cuisine. The intimate setting, friendly service, and creative menu make it a favorite among locals.

Signature Dishes:

- Roasted Chicken: Juicy and flavorful, served with seasonal vegetables.

- Pappardelle Pasta: Fresh house-made pasta with rich, savory sauces.

- Butterscotch Pot de Crème: A decadent dessert perfect for ending the meal.

Gemma's inviting atmosphere and commitment to high-quality ingredients ensure a delightful dining experience for all who visit.

Kenny’s Wood Fired Grill

Location: Addison

Kenny’s Wood Fired Grill in Addison is known for its wood-grilled steaks and seafood. The restaurant offers a warm, inviting atmosphere with a classic American steakhouse vibe.

Signature Dishes:

- Ribeye Steak: Grilled to perfection with a rich, smoky flavor.

- Lobster Deviled Eggs: A unique twist on a classic appetizer.

- The “Adult” Mac & Cheese: Creamy and indulgent, with a hint of truffle.

Kenny’s Wood Fired Grill is a great spot for casual dining and special occasions. Its menu caters to various tastes.

Petra and the Beast

Location: East Dallas

Petra and the Beast is a farm-to-table restaurant emphasizing sustainable and locally sourced ingredients. Chef Misti Norris creates innovative dishes highlighting the best seasonal produce and meats.

Signature Dishes:

- Charcuterie Board: A selection of house-cured meats and artisanal cheeses.

- Handmade Pasta: Fresh pasta dishes that change with the seasons.

- Smoked Rabbit: Tender and flavorful, with a perfect balance of smoke and spice.

The rustic setting and ever-changing menu make Petra and the Beast a unique dining destination that food enthusiasts won't miss.

The Rustic

Location: Uptown

The Rustic is a lively spot known for its farm-to-table American fare and live music. The spacious outdoor patio and casual, welcoming atmosphere make it a popular gathering place.

Signature Dishes:

- Drunk Chick: Beer-can chicken served with jalapeño spoon bread.

- Texas Hot Chicken: Spicy, crispy fried chicken with a Texas twist.

- Rustic Burger: Juicy and flavorful, topped with all the fixings.

The Rustic's combination of great food, live music, and a vibrant ambiance make it an ideal spot for a fun night out with friends or family.

Boulevardier

Location: Bishop Arts District

Boulevardier is a charming French bistro offering classic French dishes with a Southern twist. The cozy, intimate setting and attentive service create a delightful dining experience.

Signature Dishes:

- Escargots: Classic snails in garlic butter, a French staple.

- Duck Confit: Crispy on the outside, tender and flavorful on the inside.

- Bouillabaisse: A hearty seafood stew with a rich, flavorful broth.

Boulevardier's blend of French elegance and Southern hospitality makes it a favorite for those seeking a sophisticated yet comfortable dining experience.

José

Location: Lovers Lane

José is a contemporary Mexican restaurant focusing on dishes from the Jalisco region of Mexico. The stylish setting and vibrant flavors make it a standout in the Dallas dining scene.

Signature Dishes:

- Birria Tacos: Rich, savory tacos served with a flavorful consommé.

- Enchiladas Suizas: Creamy and delicious, with a perfect balance of flavors.

- Elote: Grilled Mexican street corn with cotija cheese and spices.

José's commitment to authentic flavors and fresh ingredients ensures a memorable meal for all who visit.

The Capital Grille

Location: Uptown

The Capital Grille is an upscale steakhouse known for its dry-aged steaks and refined dining experience. The elegant setting and impeccable service make it a top choice for special occasions.

Signature Dishes:

- Dry-Aged NY Strip: Tender and flavorful, aged to perfection.

- Lobster Mac ‘n’ Cheese: A decadent and indulgent side dish.

- Porcini-Rubbed Delmonico: Rich and savory, with a porcini mushroom crust.

The Capital Grille's luxurious atmosphere and top-quality cuisine ensure a dining experience that is both sophisticated and satisfying.

Knife Dallas

Location: Highland Dallas Hotel

Knife Dallas, created by celebrity chef John Tesar, offers a modern take on the classic steakhouse. The restaurant emphasizes high-quality, locally sourced meats and innovative cooking techniques.

Signature Dishes:

- Dry-Aged Steaks: A variety of cuts aged in-house for enhanced flavor.

- Bacon Sampler: An assortment of house-cured bacon with unique flavors.

- Pork Belly: Tender and flavorful, with a perfect balance of fat and meat.

Knife Dallas's commitment to quality and creativity makes it a standout destination for steak lovers and food enthusiasts.

Cultural and Historical Sites

Dallas Museum of Art

The Dallas Museum of Art (DMA) is a cultural cornerstone of the city. With a collection spanning 5,000 years of history, the museum offers a diverse range of art from around the world. Highlights include works by renowned artists such as Monet, Van Gogh, and O'Keeffe. The museum also hosts rotating exhibitions, educational programs, and special events. Admission to the permanent collection is free, making it accessible to all residents and visitors.

Nasher Sculpture Center

The Nasher Sculpture Center, located in the Dallas Arts District, is dedicated to modern and contemporary sculpture. The museum's collection features works by artists like Picasso, Rodin, and Giacometti, set in both indoor galleries and an outdoor garden. The tranquil setting provides a perfect backdrop for appreciating these masterful sculptures. The Nasher also offers educational programs, artist talks, and special exhibitions, making it a vibrant part of Dallas's cultural scene.

Dallas Heritage Village

Dallas Heritage Village offers a step back in time to explore the city’s pioneer and Victorian past. This living history museum features historic buildings and artifacts from the 19th and early 20th centuries. Visitors can explore restored homes, a schoolhouse, a church, and various businesses, all staffed by costumed interpreters. The village hosts numerous events throughout the year, including historical reenactments, making it an engaging and educational experience for all ages.

Bishop Arts District

The Bishop Arts District is a lively neighborhood known for its eclectic mix of boutiques, art galleries, restaurants, and bars. The area’s historic charm and vibrant atmosphere make it a popular destination for both locals and visitors. Art lovers will appreciate the galleries showcasing local talent, while foodies can enjoy a diverse range of culinary options. Regular events, such as the First Thursday Wine Walk, add to the district’s appeal.

George W. Bush Presidential Library and Museum

Located on the campus of Southern Methodist University, the George W. Bush Presidential Library and Museum offers an in-depth look at the life and presidency of George W. Bush. The museum features exhibits on key events of his administration, including 9/11 and the Iraq War, as well as personal artifacts and interactive displays. The museum's design and the surrounding grounds provide a dignified setting for reflection and learning.

Meadows Museum

The Meadows Museum, also located on the SMU campus, is home to one of the largest collections of Spanish art outside of Spain. The museum's extensive collection includes works by El Greco, Velázquez, Goya, and Picasso. The elegant galleries and beautifully landscaped grounds enhance the experience of viewing these masterpieces. The Meadows also hosts temporary exhibitions, educational programs, and cultural events, making it a valuable resource for art lovers.

Event Calendar for New Residents

Dallas Blooms at the Arboretum

Dallas Blooms is an annual spring festival held at the Dallas Arboretum and Botanical Garden. It features one of the largest floral displays in the Southwest, with over 500,000 blooming flowers, including tulips, daffodils, and azaleas. The event includes live music, cooking demonstrations, and special activities for children. It's a wonderful way to welcome the new season and explore the beauty of the arboretum.

State Fair of Texas

The State Fair of Texas, held annually at Fair Park, is a beloved tradition that attracts millions of visitors. The fair features a wide range of attractions, including carnival rides, live music, livestock shows, and the iconic Texas Star Ferris wheel. Food is a major highlight, with vendors offering everything from classic corn dogs to innovative fried treats. The fair provides a unique blend of entertainment, culture, and Texas heritage.

Dallas International Film Festival

The Dallas International Film Festival showcases a diverse selection of films from around the world. Held annually, the festival features screenings of feature films, documentaries, shorts, and student films. In addition to film screenings, the festival hosts panel discussions, Q&A sessions with filmmakers, and special events. It's a fantastic opportunity for film enthusiasts to explore new works and engage with the filmmaking community.

AT&T Performing Arts Center's Broadway Series

The AT&T Performing Arts Center hosts a Broadway Series that brings top touring productions to Dallas. The series includes popular musicals, plays, and special performances, offering a taste of Broadway right in the heart of Texas. The state-of-the-art venues, such as the Winspear Opera House and the Wyly Theatre, provide an exceptional setting for these world-class performances. Subscriptions and single tickets are available, making it easy for new residents to enjoy top-tier theater.

Taste of Dallas

Taste of Dallas is an annual food festival that celebrates the city's diverse culinary scene. Held in various locations, the event features tastings from local restaurants, food trucks, and chefs. Visitors can enjoy live cooking demonstrations, music, and entertainment while sampling a wide array of dishes. It's a great way for new residents to discover the flavors of Dallas and connect with the local food community.

Deep Ellum Arts Festival

The Deep Ellum Arts Festival is a three-day event that transforms the historic Deep Ellum neighborhood into a vibrant celebration of art and music. The festival features hundreds of visual artists, live music performances on multiple stages, and a variety of food and drink vendors. The eclectic mix of art, music, and culture reflects the unique spirit of Deep Ellum, making it a must-visit event for new residents.

Dallas Pride

Dallas Pride is an annual celebration of the LGBTQ+ community, featuring a parade, festival, and various events throughout the city. The parade, held in Fair Park, showcases colorful floats, live music, and performances, while the festival offers food, drinks, and entertainment. Dallas Pride is a vibrant and inclusive event that promotes diversity and equality, welcoming everyone to join in the celebration.

North Texas Irish Festival

The North Texas Irish Festival, held at Fair Park, is a lively celebration of Irish culture and heritage. The festival features live music, dance performances, traditional Irish food, and family-friendly activities. Attendees can enjoy performances by local and international artists, participate in workshops, and explore vendor booths offering Irish crafts and goods. It's a festive event that brings the spirit of Ireland to Dallas.

Dallas St. Patrick's Parade & Festival

The Dallas St. Patrick's Parade & Festival is one of the largest St. Patrick's Day celebrations in the country. The parade runs along Greenville Avenue and features colorful floats, marching bands, and performers. The festival includes live music, food, drinks, and family-friendly activities. The event creates a festive atmosphere, celebrating Irish culture and community spirit.

Dallas Symphony Orchestra Concerts

The Dallas Symphony Orchestra (DSO) offers a wide range of concerts throughout the year, from classical symphonies to contemporary performances. The DSO performs at the Morton H. Meyerson Symphony Center, known for its excellent acoustics and beautiful architecture. New residents can enjoy subscription series, single concert tickets, and special events, providing numerous opportunities to experience world-class music in Dallas

This comprehensive guide covers a wide array of activities and attractions in Dallas, ensuring that new residents have a wealth of options to explore and enjoy. Each section offers detailed insights into what makes Dallas a vibrant and welcoming city, helping newcomers feel right at home.

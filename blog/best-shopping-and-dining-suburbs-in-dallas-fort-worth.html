The Best Shopping and Dining Suburbs in Dallas Fort Worth

Kristen Carpentier Licensed TX Realtor®  ·  June 4, 2024  ·  9 min read

When considering a move to the Dallas-Fort Worth (DFW) area, one of the key factors that attract homebuyers is the availability of excellent shopping and dining options. The metroplex boasts a variety of suburbs, each offering unique retail and culinary experiences. Whether you're a shopping enthusiast, a food lover, or someone who enjoys vibrant nightlife, DFW has a suburb that fits your lifestyle. In this detailed guide, we'll explore the best suburbs in the DFW area for shopping and dining.

Cities with Premier Shopping Districts

Plano Texas

Plano is home to some of the most upscale shopping districts in the DFW area. The Shops at Legacy and Legacy West are premier destinations featuring high-end boutiques, national retailers, and a variety of dining options. The Shops at Legacy offers an open-air environment with a mix of popular brands like Kendra Scott and specialty stores. Legacy West, on the other hand, provides a luxurious shopping experience with stores like Louis Vuitton, Gucci, and Tiffany & Co. Additionally, both centers host numerous events throughout the year, including fashion shows, concerts, and food festivals, adding to the vibrant atmosphere.

Frisco Texas

Frisco is another top choice for shopping aficionados. Stonebriar Centre is one of the largest malls in North Texas, offering over 200 stores, including Nordstrom, Macy’s, and a vast array of specialty shops. Beyond the mall, Frisco Square and The Star provide excellent retail options and dining experiences. Frisco Square combines shopping, dining, and entertainment in a walkable area, featuring local boutiques and popular chains. The Star, home to the Dallas Cowboys World Headquarters, includes high-end retail stores, dining options, and entertainment venues, making it a comprehensive destination for shoppers.

Southlake Texas

Southlake Town Square is a crown jewel in the suburb of Southlake. This open-air shopping center features a blend of high-end retailers like Apple, Anthropologie, and J.Crew, alongside local boutiques and gourmet dining options. The walkable layout and charming atmosphere make it an ideal spot for a day of shopping and dining. Southlake Town Square also hosts community events, such as outdoor concerts and holiday celebrations, enhancing the shopping experience with a sense of community.

Top Cities for Restaurants and Cafes

Addison Texas

Addison is renowned for its vibrant dining scene. With over 200 restaurants in just a few square miles, it’s a haven for food lovers. Addison Circle Park is the epicenter of this culinary diversity, offering a range of options from upscale dining at places like Al Biernat's, known for its prime steaks and extensive wine list, to the casual atmosphere of cafes like Astoria Caffe, a cozy spot for coffee and pastries. Addison’s dining options also include a variety of international cuisines, from authentic Italian at Antonio Ristorante to delectable sushi at Mr. Sushi.

Grapevine Texas

Grapevine combines historic charm with a contemporary dining scene. Main Street is lined with a variety of eateries, from fine dining establishments like Winewood Grill, which offers a menu of contemporary American cuisine and an extensive wine list, to cozy cafes such as Main Street Bistro & Bakery, known for its delicious brunch and pastries. The city’s annual food and wine festivals, like Grapevine’s Main Street Fest and GrapeFest, further enhance its reputation as a culinary hotspot, attracting food lovers from all over.

Richardson Texas

Richardson is known for its multicultural dining options. The city boasts an array of ethnic restaurants, including some of the best Asian cuisine in the DFW area. For instance, Jeng Chi offers an authentic Taiwanese dining experience with its famous soup dumplings, while Haystack Burgers & Barley provides a classic American comfort food experience with gourmet burgers and craft beers. Richardson’s diversity is also reflected in its numerous Indian, Mediterranean, and Mexican restaurants, ensuring that there’s something for everyone.

Top Cities for Farmers Markets and Specialty Stores

McKinney Texas

McKinney’s Historic Downtown hosts a vibrant farmers market that attracts locals and visitors alike. The Chestnut Square Farmers Market offers fresh, locally-sourced produce, artisanal goods, and specialty items. Vendors provide a wide array of products, including organic vegetables, homemade jams, fresh-baked bread, and handcrafted soaps. The historic setting adds to the charm, making it a perfect weekend outing. Additionally, McKinney’s downtown area features numerous specialty stores and boutiques, offering unique items and supporting local artisans.

Fort Worth Texas

Fort Worth's Clearfork Farmers Market and Cowtown Farmers Market are popular destinations for fresh produce and local specialties. Clearfork Farmers Market, located in the upscale Clearfork development, offers a selection of high-quality produce, meats, and artisanal goods. Cowtown Farmers Market, the city's longest-running farmers market, emphasizes local and sustainable products, offering everything from organic vegetables to free-range eggs. Fort Worth’s commitment to supporting local farmers and artisans is evident in the quality and variety of products available at these markets.

Coppell Texas

Coppell's Farmers Market is a community favorite, offering fresh produce, handmade goods, and specialty foods. The market features a wide range of vendors, providing everything from seasonal fruits and vegetables to artisanal cheeses and local honey. The market is known for its friendly atmosphere and high-quality offerings, making it a must-visit for anyone interested in local, sustainable products. Coppell also hosts various events and workshops, promoting healthy living and community engagement.

Top Cities for Nightlife and Entertainment

Dallas (Uptown)

Uptown Dallas is the epicenter of nightlife and entertainment in the city. The area is packed with trendy bars, lively nightclubs, and entertainment venues. Popular spots like The Rustic offer live music and a laid-back atmosphere with a large outdoor patio. Concrete Cowboy provides a vibrant nightlife experience with DJ performances and a dance floor. Uptown’s entertainment options also include comedy clubs, rooftop bars, and chic lounges, ensuring there’s something for everyone.

Arlington Texas

Arlington is home to the famous AT&T Stadium and Globe Life Field, making it a hub for sports and entertainment. The Texas Live! complex is a dynamic destination, offering a mix of restaurants, bars, and live music venues. Places like Troy’s, named after NFL Hall of Famer Troy Aikman, provide a unique dining and entertainment experience with live music and a vibrant atmosphere. Arlington’s entertainment options extend beyond sports, with venues like Arlington Music Hall offering concerts and performances.

Fort Worth (Sundance Square)

Sundance Square in Fort Worth is a bustling entertainment district featuring theaters, live music venues, and a variety of bars and restaurants. The area’s historic charm is complemented by modern amenities, creating a unique nightlife experience. Popular venues include Bass Performance Hall, which hosts Broadway shows and concerts, and Scat Jazz Lounge, known for its intimate setting and live jazz performances. Sundance Square’s lively atmosphere and diverse entertainment options make it a favorite destination for both locals and visitors.

Top Overall Suburbs of Dallas with the Best Shopping and Dining

Highland Park Texas

Highland Park offers an upscale shopping and dining experience that is hard to match. Highland Park Village, one of the country’s first shopping centers, features luxury brands like Chanel, Hermès, and Dior. The shopping center also boasts gourmet restaurants such as Bistro 31, known for its European-inspired cuisine and elegant ambiance. The elegant atmosphere and exclusive offerings make Highland Park a top choice for discerning shoppers and foodies. The suburb’s tree-lined streets and historic homes add to its charm, making it a highly desirable place to live.

Allen Texas

Allen's Fairview Town Center and The Village at Allen provide a comprehensive shopping and dining experience. Fairview Town Center features a mix of well-known retailers, specialty shops, and a diverse range of dining options, from casual eateries like Gloria’s Latin Cuisine to fine dining at Wild Salsa. The Village at Allen offers additional retail and dining choices, with stores like HomeGoods and eateries like BJ’s Restaurant & Brewhouse. Allen’s family-friendly environment and quality amenities make it an attractive option for homebuyers.

Colleyville Texas

Colleyville’s Town Center offers a mix of boutique shopping and gourmet dining. The suburb is known for its charming atmosphere and high-quality retail and culinary options. Boutique stores like Sassy Flamingo and Market Street provide unique shopping experiences, while Next Bistro offers gourmet dining focusing on fresh, local ingredients. With its close-knit community feel and excellent schools, Colleyville is an excellent choice for families and individuals seeking a refined suburban lifestyle.

Irving (Las Colinas)

Las Colinas in Irving combines business with leisure, offering excellent shopping and dining options. The Toyota Music Factory is a central hub, featuring a variety of restaurants, bars, and entertainment venues. Restaurants like Thirsty Lion Gastropub & Grill provide a lively dining experience with a diverse menu and craft beers. The Water Street development adds to the appeal with its scenic views of Lake Carolyn and trendy eateries like Hugo’s Invitados, known for its modern Mexican cuisine. Las Colinas’s mix of business and leisure makes it a dynamic place to live.

Flower Mound Texas

Flower Mound’s Lakeside area provides a mix of shopping and dining experiences. Lakeside DFW or Lakeside Village offers a blend of retail shops, restaurants, and entertainment options with scenic views of Grapevine Lake. Restaurants like Mio Nonno Trattoria offer delicious Italian cuisine with a view and a phenomenal steak house, 1845 has dishes to die for, making it a great place for dining. There is also Tavern at Lakeside, known for its inviting atmosphere and diverse menu. Flower Mound’s family-friendly environment and quality amenities make it a great place to live, shop, and dine.

The Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex offers a plethora of suburbs, each with its unique appeal for shopping and dining. Whether you’re looking for luxury brands, diverse culinary options, or vibrant nightlife, there’s a suburb in DFW that caters to your needs.
